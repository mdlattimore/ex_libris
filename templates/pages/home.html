Welcome to My Literary Collection

This is a personal catalog of the books I collect with special interest—volumes that go beyond casual reading and reflect a deeper connection to certain authors and their worlds. Here you'll find works by J.R.R. Tolkien and Charles Dickens, as well as related books that explore their lives, influence, and the broader literary landscapes they helped shape. It’s not a complete library, but a curated space for the titles that matter most to me.

In the collections, you'll find a few of what I consider "collectible" pieces, editions with some special significance. Among these include 5 first editions of Tolkien's The Silmarillion (my wife says I have a problem) and multiple copies of Dickens' A Christmas Carol (none of which are valuable in any monetary sense, but make up various presentations of one of my favorite stories of all time).

Select books have what I call "Spotlight" pages: deeper dives into the more significant works. You can look at those Spotlights by following the links below or by following the link found on the individual edition pages.
